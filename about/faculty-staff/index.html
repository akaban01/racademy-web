Our Standard

What it takes to teach here.

Hiring standard

Every faculty member is degreed in their subject area, and certified or trained where applicable. Beyond credentials, every hire goes through a background check and is trained in both our academic standards and our approach to tarbiyah - the deliberate, daily work of character formation that runs through every grade.

Departments

The teams behind the classroom.

Academics

Early Childhood & Elementary

Homeroom and subject teachers for Pre-K through 5th grade, focused on foundational literacy, math, and a love of learning.

Academics

Middle & High School

Subject-area teachers across math, science, English, history, and electives - including our AP program faculty.

Faith

Qur'an & Islamic Studies

Dedicated, trained Qur'an and Islamic Studies teachers - not classroom teachers doubling up on a side subject.

Language

Arabic

Arabic language faculty teaching Pre-K through 12th grade as a living language, not a once-a-week elective.

Support

Student Support

Staff who support students academically and personally - coordinating with teachers and families when extra help is needed.

Operations

Administration

The team that keeps the school running day to day - admissions, front office, facilities, and school leadership.
