A Second Campus

Built for the families on our waiting list.

Renaissance Academy is growing into a 19-acre Pre-K through 12th grade campus in Leander, just north of Austin - built to serve a waiting list of more than 200 students our current campus can't accommodate.

Overview Mission & Vision Renaissance FAQs Careers Governance Campus New Campus Contact Us
Why We're Building

Demand has outgrown one campus.

The need

Austin's Muslim community has grown quickly, and so has demand for a school that pairs strong academics with daily Islamic education. Our current campus is at capacity, with over 200 students on the waiting list - families we currently can't accommodate. The new campus exists to change that.

The Site

19 acres in Leander, Texas.

Location

Leander, north of Austin

650 Private Road 920, Leander, TX - chosen for accessibility from both the western and eastern parts of the Greater Austin community.

Scope

Pre-K through 12th grade

A single site built to hold the full Renaissance program from Pre-K through high school graduation, plus space for community-facing services.

Getting there

A bus system is planned

We know a new location means a different commute for some families - a bus system is part of the plan to keep the new campus accessible.
