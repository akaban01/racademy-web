About The School

The basics, answered.

What grades does Renaissance serve?
Pre-K through 12th grade, on one campus in North Austin. Most students who start with us in Pre-K or Kindergarten stay through high school graduation.
Is Renaissance accredited?
Yes - Renaissance is accredited with distinction by Cognia, and holds SACS and CASI accreditation as well. Credits and diplomas transfer the way they would from any accredited school.
What does the school calendar look like?
A standard two-semester calendar with the usual fall, winter, and spring breaks, plus accommodations around the two Eids. The full calendar is shared with enrolled families and posted in Parent Resources each year.
Do students wear uniforms?
Yes, every grade band has a standard uniform. Specifics and where to order them are shared once a family enrolls, and printable details also live in Parent Resources.
What languages are part of the curriculum?
English is the language of instruction across academic subjects. Arabic is taught daily from Pre-K through 12th grade as a living language, alongside daily Qur'an instruction.
How big are the classes?
We keep class sizes small by design, so teachers can know every student by name. Exact ratios vary slightly by grade band - ask during a tour and we'll give you the current numbers for your child's grade.
Is before- or after-school care available?
Yes, an extended-day option is available for families who need earlier drop-off or later pickup. Ask the office for current hours and how to enroll.
How can parents get involved?
Through classroom volunteering, school events, and the parent community that forms naturally around a small campus. There's no shortage of ways to pitch in - just let your child's teacher or the office know you're interested.
