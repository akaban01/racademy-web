How we're run

Stewardship that outlasts any one leader.

Renaissance is governed by a volunteer Board of Trustees that sets direction and holds the school accountable, and led day to day by school leadership who carry that direction into the classroom.

Structure

Two layers, one direction.

Governs

Board of Trustees

A volunteer board that sets strategic direction, approves the annual budget, and holds fiduciary and policy oversight of the school. The board hires, supports, and evaluates the Head of School.

Leads

Head of School & Leadership Team

Responsible for day-to-day operations, academics, hiring, and the lived experience of every student and family on campus - reporting to and accountable to the board.

Oversees

Committees

Standing committees - covering areas like finance, academics, and facilities - do focused work between board meetings and bring recommendations back to the full board.

Accountability

Why governance matters here.

Our approach

A school can drift without structure to hold it accountable. The board exists so that decisions about budget, leadership, and long-term direction aren't made by any one person in isolation - and so that the mission outlasts any individual's tenure. Outside accreditation through Cognia, SACS, and CASI adds a further layer of independent review.

Get Involved

How families can engage.

Stay informed, speak up

Governance works best with informed families.

Major decisions - calendar changes, tuition adjustments, new programs - are communicated to families well ahead of time. If you have a question or concern that belongs in front of the board, the office can help route it appropriately.

Have a question for leadership?

Reach out and we'll make sure it gets to the right person.
