Our School

Rooted in the community, built for the future.

Located in the heart of Austin, Texas, Renaissance Academy has served the Greater Austin community since 2007 — providing Pre-K through College-Prep High School with a 100% graduation rate. Our graduates have been admitted to Tier 1 colleges including UC Berkeley, UT Austin, and Texas A&M, supported by our Advanced Placement program and technology-rich classrooms.

What We Stand For

Three pillars, one purpose.

Everything we build rests on the same foundation — faith, knowledge, and character, woven together rather than taught apart.

Qur'an & Deen

A strong, joyful grounding in the Qur'an, the Sunnah, and the foundations of our faith — taught in a way students carry with them for life.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous, well-rounded education built on Texas (TEKS) standards and AP coursework that prepares students to thrive in any path they choose.

Character & Community

An environment where adab, sincerity, and leadership are practiced daily — and where families feel part of one extended community.

Our Academy

A complete journey, Pre-K through High.

Each stage builds on the last — academically, spiritually, and personally — guiding students from their first words to college admission.

Ages 2–4

Pre-K

A leading provider of early childhood education in North Austin, with age-appropriate programs for PreK-2, PreK-3, and PreK-4 — introducing literacy, numeracy, and Islamic values through joyful learning.

Grades K–5

Elementary School

State-of-the-art primary education paired with Islamic studies, taught by teachers trained in the cognitive and psychological development of every child.

Grades 6–8

Middle School

The TEKS curriculum standards strengthened with a robust program in Arabic Language, Quran, and Islamic Studies — building critical thinkers and confident young Muslims.

Grades 9–12

High School

A balanced curriculum meeting Texas graduation requirements, with AP courses and dedicated college counseling — the opportunity to go above and beyond.
