Support Renaissance Academy

Give knowing exactly what it builds.

In Islam, giving is described as a means of purifying wealth and drawing closer to Allah. Every gift to Renaissance Academy goes toward one of three things: a permanent home for the school, a seat for a student who couldn't otherwise afford one, or the daily operations that keep our classrooms running.
