Payment plan

Spread annual tuition across the school year with automatic monthly payments through FACTS — the same provider many private schools use. Annual and semi-annual options are also available.

Common Questions

About tuition, specifically.

Why isn't tuition listed on the website?
Tuition varies by grade band and can shift slightly year to year, and we'd rather explain it with context — what's included, payment options, sibling considerations — than post a number that gets read in isolation. It takes one email or call to get a straight answer.
Is the application fee separate from tuition?
Yes. The application fee covers processing your family's file and is paid once, during the application step. It's separate from — and not credited toward — annual tuition.
How does the sibling adjustment work?
Families with more than one child enrolled at the same time receive an adjustment on tuition for each additional sibling. The exact amount is reviewed each year — we'll cover the current figure during your family conversation.
How do I apply for financial assistance?
Need-based assistance is handled separately and confidentially from the admissions decision itself. Email the office to request the application — awards are limited and reviewed on a rolling basis, so it's worth asking early.
What happens if a payment is missed?
Reach out to the office as soon as you anticipate an issue — we'd much rather work out a plan with you than let it become a bigger problem. Life happens, and we try to treat families like family.
