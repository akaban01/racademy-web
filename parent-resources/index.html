First-time setup

Follow these steps to create your ParentsWeb account. Make sure the school has your email address on file in RenWeb before you start.

  1. Open the ParentsWeb login page

    Go to ra-tx.client.renweb.com/pw/ - or, from racademy.org, choose Admissions → Parent Resources → ParentsWeb.

  2. Click "Create New ParentsWeb Account"

    You'll find the link below the username and password fields on the login page.

  3. Enter the District Code and your email

    District Code is RA-TX. Use the same email address you gave on the registration form. Then click Create Account.

  4. Check your email and set your password

    An email is sent with a "Click to change password" link. The link is valid for 30 minutes. Open it, set a User Name and Password, then click Save Password.

  5. Log in

    You'll land on the School Information home page. Click Tour the New ParentsWeb to watch a 6-minute video tour of the portal.

Frequently asked questions

What is the ParentsWeb website address?
Type ra-tx.client.renweb.com/pw/ into your browser's address bar, or use the "Open ParentsWeb" button above.
I'm seeing "Error: Invalid Credentials." How do I log in?
The fastest fix is to reset your credentials:
  1. On the login page, click Forgot Username/Password.
  2. Enter your email address - it must match the email the school has in RenWeb.
  3. Open the email and click the "Click to change password" link (valid for 30 minutes).
  4. You'll see your Name, Person ID, User Name, and an obscured Password. Type a new User Name and Password, then click Save Password.
  5. A confirmation message appears at the top of the browser. Log in with your new credentials.
I don't know my username. How do I log in?
Click Forgot Username/Password and enter the email address the school has on file. The email you receive will display your existing User Name. If you remember your Password, you can simply close the page and log in with your User Name and Password - no need to reset.
I'm a new parent. How do I get a username and password?
First, make sure the school has your email address on file in RenWeb. Then:
  1. Go to ra-tx.client.renweb.com/pw/.
  2. The District Code is filled in automatically. If not, enter RA-TX.
  3. Click Create New ParentsWeb Account.
  4. Type your email address - an email is sent to you.
  5. Click the "Click to change password" link in the email (valid for 30 minutes).
  6. The browser displays your Name and RenWeb ID. Type a User Name, Password, and Confirm Password, then click Save Password.
  7. You'll see "User Name/Password successfully updated." Log in with your new credentials.
Keep your account secure. Your login gives access to your child's records - including grades, attendance, and discipline. Please do not share your username or password with others, and use a strong, unique password.

About FACTS

FACTS is Renaissance Academy's tuition management system. Setting up a FACTS payment plan lets you spread tuition across the school year with automatic payments from a financial account you choose. You'll set everything up from inside your ParentsWeb account.

What you'll need

An active ParentsWeb account
Financial account information (bank or card)
About 10 minutes to complete setup

Don't have a ParentsWeb account yet? Follow the ParentsWeb setup instructions first →

Setting up your payment plan

Once you're signed in to ParentsWeb, these eight steps will walk you through the FACTS setup wizard.

  1. Sign in to ParentsWeb

    Log in at ra-tx.client.renweb.com/pw/ using your ParentsWeb credentials.

  2. Open the Financial tab

    From the left side menu, click FINANCIAL.

  3. Choose "Set up a Payment Plan"

    In the Financial Links box, select Set up a Payment Plan.

  4. Click Begin

    Click Begin to launch the FACTS setup wizard.

  5. Plan Options

    Select a payment plan and click Next. Take a moment to review any fee information before continuing.

  6. Payment Details

    Enter your financial account information - this is where your tuition payments will be drawn from.

  7. Payment Schedule

    Pick your payment date and review the full schedule of upcoming charges.

  8. Review & Authorize

    Use the Change links on the right to go back and edit any entry. When everything looks correct, check the box to agree to the Terms & Conditions and click Done.

Need help mid-setup? Call a FACTS representative directly at (866) 441-4637. For Renaissance-specific questions about your tuition balance or plan options, please reach out to the school office at contact@racademy.org or 512-252-2277.

About transcripts

Renaissance Academy issues official and unofficial student transcripts for college and university applications, transfers between schools, scholarship and financial-aid programs, and employment or eligibility verification. Requests are handled by the school office.

How to request

Email is the fastest path - use the button above to start a pre-filled request. Or follow these steps to make a request by phone or in person.

  1. Contact the school office

    Email contact@racademy.org, call 512-252-2277, or stop by during office hours.

  2. Share student details

    Provide the student's full legal name, date of birth, and graduation year (or current grade). This helps the office locate the correct record quickly.

  3. Specify the type of transcript

    Let us know whether you need an official sealed copy, an electronic copy sent directly to a receiving institution, or an unofficial copy for personal records.

  4. Tell us where it should go

    For each destination, include the full mailing address or the institution's transcript-receiving email. Official transcripts are typically sent directly from the school to the receiving institution.

  5. Allow processing time

    Please allow several business days for the office to prepare and send transcripts. For time-sensitive deadlines (college applications, scholarships), reach out as early as possible.

What to include in your request

Student's full legal name
Date of birth
Graduation year or current grade
Transcript type (official / electronic / unofficial)
Number of copies needed
Destination address or email
Requester name & relationship
Contact phone number
Deadline (if applicable)
Need it urgently or have a special situation? If you're approaching a college, scholarship, or transfer deadline - or you need a transcript for a former student - call the office at 512-252-2277 so we can help right away.
