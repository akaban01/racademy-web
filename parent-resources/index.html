Student supply lists.

Find your child's grade below and tap to view the required supplies. Please label all items with your child's name using a permanent marker.

A few notes for everyone: Please send only what is on your child's list — extra items take up classroom space. No rolling backpacks at most grade levels (see specific lists). Items marked with a clear front pocket should not be paper folders. If you have questions about a specific item, the office is happy to help.

Tip: Tap the checkboxes to mark items off as you shop — your progress is saved on this device. Want a paper copy? Use the print buttons below or on any grade.
