Rigorous academics, no asterisk.

Renaissance students cover the same core subjects as any strong college-prep school - held to an accredited standard, taught by degreed subject teachers, and built to prepare students for selective high schools and colleges.

Core Subjects

What every student studies.

Core

Math & Science

Sequenced math from foundational arithmetic through Calculus, and lab-based science building toward AP-level coursework in high school.

Core

English & Language Arts

Reading, writing, and critical thinking developed year over year, from early literacy through high school composition and literature.

Core

History & Social Studies

World and U.S. history, geography, and civics - taught alongside Islamic history so students see where their own story fits.

Language

Arabic

Daily Arabic instruction from Pre-K through 12th grade, taught as a living language alongside Qur'anic Arabic.

Standards

Held to an outside bar.

Accreditation

Renaissance is accredited with distinction by Cognia, and holds SACS and CASI accreditation as well - independent review that keeps our academics honest and our diplomas and credits transferable, the same as any accredited school.

College preparation

AP coursework and a college-prep track through 12th grade.
