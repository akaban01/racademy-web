Room to go deeper.

Beyond the core school day, students can go further into what they love — memorizing Qur'an, building robots, running for student council, or just finding their people. Clubs run by grade band and change from year to year based on student interest.

How It Works

Built around real student interest.

Each year is different

Clubs and activities are offered by grade band and shift from year to year based on which students sign up and which staff are available to sponsor them. Families hear specifics — meeting times, sign-up details, and any seasonal offerings — directly from the school once the year is underway.
