Curriculum

What students learn.

A traditional, rigorous American elementary curriculum — paired with the most substantial Qur'an & Arabic program of any private school in the area.

Core academics

  • ELA — reading, writing, grammar, vocabulary
  • Mathematics — conceptual + fluency-based
  • Science — hands-on inquiry and investigation
  • Social Studies — Texas, U.S., and world history
  • Library & research skills from Grade 2

Qur'an, Arabic & Islamic Studies

  • Daily Qur'an with leveled small groups
  • Tajweed and memorization (Juz 'Amma and beyond)
  • Arabic — reading, writing, and conversation
  • Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ across grade levels
  • Salah, fiqh basics, and adab built into daily life

Specials & enrichment

  • Physical education and movement
  • Art — drawing, painting, calligraphy
  • Library and read-aloud blocks
  • Technology and digital citizenship
  • Field trips, science fair, Qur'an competitions
